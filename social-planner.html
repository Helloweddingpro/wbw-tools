Instagram: Post your 10–15 sec venue video as a Reel. Use the venue name and location in the first line of your caption. Tag the venue and every supplier visible in the clip.

Facebook: Share the Reel directly to your Facebook page — don't cross-post from Instagram as reach is lower. Tag the venue's Facebook page and any suppliers. Also share in local wedding groups.

TikTok: Hook in the first 2 seconds — start mid-action. Use the venue name and location as hashtags and in the caption. Tag the venue if they have a TikTok account.
