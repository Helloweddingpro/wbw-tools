💡 What is a conversion rate?

Your conversion rate is the percentage of enquiries that turn into actual bookings. If 10 couples enquire and you book 3, your conversion rate is 30%.

For most wedding suppliers a healthy rate is between 25% and 40%. If yours is lower it is usually a sign that your enquiry response or follow-up process needs attention — not your prices.

Enter your enquiries and bookings above to see your rate.

The higher your conversion rate, the fewer enquiries you need to reach your goal — which means less time chasing leads and more time doing the work you love.
